  • THE QUEST FUTSAL FEST 2020 (5 A SIDE STATE LEVEL FOOTBALL TOURNAMENT)

    State level QUEST FUTSAL Fest 2020 was held during 7th, 9th and 10th of February at QUEST International School, Tirupur with around 32 teams from the schools across Tamilnadu participating. Each team consisted of 10 members each under the categories of under 8 and 10 respectively. It was a hard-fought battle as all the teams were vying for the coveted Champions trophy.

  • TIPS ERODE CHILDREN CROWNED CHAMPIONS IN KOVAI ARCHERY

    Our budding archery stars won 6 Gold, 3 Silver, 2 Bronze and proudly bagged the Overall Championship, in the Kovai Archery Championship held on 23rd February 2020. The event was organized by Eagle archery academy, Coimbatore. More than 50 Schools from South-zone participated in this event and our students competed strenuously to secure the championship.

  • TIPS KIDS RAISED FUNDS FOR NGO FOR A BEGGER FREE COMMUNITY

    As a part of the inquiry on goods & services, the students applied math and economics concepts of Profit/Loss. The consumer had been parents, students from secondary years and facilitators. The students of Gr.5 ended up with a profit of Rs.66,400 which the students decided to contribute to Atchayam Trust – an NGO which is working towards a beggar free community.
